Sourdough Bread

Description

Sourdough bread is a timeless, artisan loaf known for its distinctive tangy flavor and chewy texture. Made through a natural fermentation process, it uses wild yeast and lactic acid bacteria instead of commercial yeast, giving it a deeper, more complex taste. The result is a crusty, golden-brown exterior with a soft, airy interior full of irregular holes. The tanginess comes from the lactic acid produced by the bacteria, which also contributes to its slightly sour flavor profile. Perfect for sandwiches, toast, or served alongside a hearty soup, sourdough is a versatile and flavorful choice for bread lovers.

Ingredients

  • 120g Whole Wheat Flour
  • 480g Bread Flour
  • 480g Water
  • 12g Salt
  • 120g Sourdough Starter

Steps

  1. Mix flour and water until homogenized
  2. Rest for 30-60 minutes
  3. Gently mix in starter and salt until homogenized
  4. Rest for 30-60 minutes
  5. Stretch dough
  6. Repeat last two steps
  7. Let rise 4-6 hours
  8. Shape into loaf and place in banneton
  9. Proof for about 2 hours
  10. Place on parchment paper and score
  11. Place in Dutch oven
  12. Bake at 400 for 30 min w/ lid on
  13. Take off lid and bake for an additional 30 minutes
