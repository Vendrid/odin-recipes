Fajitas

Description

Fajitas are a vibrant and flavorful Tex-Mex dish traditionally made with grilled strips of marinated meat-commonly beef, chicken, or shrimp-served sizzling hot alongside sauteed bell peppers and onions. Typically presented on a hot skillet, fajitas are accompanied by warm flour tortillas and a variety of toppings such as guacamole, sour cream, salsa, shredded cheese, and fresh pico de gallo. Perfect for sharing, fajitas offer a customizable and interactive dining experience that blends bold spices with fresh ingredients, making them a favorite in Mexican and Southwestern cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp Chili Powder
  • 1 tbsp Ground Cumin
  • 1 tbsp Garlic Powder
  • 1 tbsp Paprika
  • 1 tbsp Oregano
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Ground Pepper
  • 2 lb Chicken
  • 2 Onions
  • 2 Bell Peppers
  • 1 tbsp Minced Garlic
  • Lime Juice
  • 1 tbsp Butter
  • Tortillas

Steps

  1. Cut chicken into strips
  2. Jullianne bell peppers and onions
  3. Mix all spices into chicken strips
  4. Cook chicken thouroughly
  5. Remove chicken from heat and add vegtables and butter
  6. Sautee on low-med heat until vegtables soften
  7. Add chicken back into pan
  8. Add a splash of lime juice
  9. Serve on tortillas
