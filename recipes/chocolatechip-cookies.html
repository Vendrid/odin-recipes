Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Chocolate chip cookies are a timeless favorite, known for their soft, chewy texture and rich, buttery flavor studded with sweet, melty chocolate chips. Whether enjoyed warm from the oven or savored as a sweet snack, these classic cookies offer the perfect balance of crispy edges and gooey centers. Made with simple ingredients like flour, sugar, eggs, and vanilla, chocolate chip cookies are loved by all ages and easily customizable with additions like nuts or different types of chocolate. They are a comforting treat that brings a sense of home with every bite.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 2 cups dark brown sugar
  • Vanilla extract
  • 4 cups flour
  • 1 tbs corn starch
  • 1 tbs baking powder
  • 1 tbs bakin soda
  • 2 tsp cream of tarter
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2-3 eggs
  • 2-3 cups chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Mix dry and wet ingredients in seperate bowls
  2. Combine into one bowl
  3. Make balls with dough
  4. Bake at 400 for 9 minutes
